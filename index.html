Don't get caught

Black Saturday + the border kit

Summer Saturdays — especially 18 & 25 Jul — are changeover hell on the arteries; Sundays are lorry-free. The whole A-vs-B choice turns on which day you hit the Alpine passes.

⚫

Black Saturday — and why it decides A vs B

FR samedi noir / DE Reisewelle / IT esodo: the peak-summer holiday changeover when Europe swaps over at once. For this route France is only NE transit (A26/A4) — not the southbound Med jam. The one real chokepoint is the Fernpass + Brenner passes.

Plan A crosses Saturday

Must go early — 06:30 start to clear the Fernpass/Brenner before the weekend rush builds. Oversleep and you sit in it.

Plan B crosses Sunday morning

Lorry-free and inherently calmer — no dawn dash. This is the structural advantage of the (booked) Friday-evening departure.

The rule either way: cross the passes weekday/Sunday-morning, never mid-Saturday. It's also the exact stop-and-go congestion type behind the family's past rear-end — worth dodging.

🎟️

Vignettes — buy before each border

Online the night before: Austria (10-day), Slovenia, Hungary (10-day D1). Germany is toll-free for cars.

🅿️

ZTL — auto-fines

Don't drive into Bolzano or Trento old-town ZTLs (cameras fine you by post). Stay on the bypass; Predazzo/Val di Fiemme is fine.

🚛

2026 HGV bans (verify near the date)

Italy: Sat 08:00–16:00, Sun 07:00–22:00. Austria: Alpine-pass weekend limits. Hungary: Sat 15:00 → Sun 22:00. Sundays are best for big motorway miles.

The call

B is booked · A is a free upgrade if the 06:30 start is realistic

Plan B (Fri 17 eve, as booked) is the safe default: the Sunday-morning Alpine crossing is lorry-free and calm, it keeps all the free school childcare and needs only ~2 leave days. The only cost is one fewer day at altitude.

Plan A (switch the ticket to Thu 16 eve) buys a full stationary Dolomites day — a second Predazzo night, coaster + Lago di Carezza unhurried — but it hinges entirely on a 06:30 Saturday start over the Fernpass. Take A only if that early start is realistic with the kids; otherwise B is the honest choice. Same route, same base arrival (Tue 21), either way.
