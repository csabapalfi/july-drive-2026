Real data · researched 11 Jul 2026

Traffic, borders & the fine print

The honest read from live sources: traffic does not decide A vs B — both dodge the one thing that really hurts (a Saturday Alpine crossing). Then the border/road traps that will bite if missed.

🚦

Traffic reality check — it's a near-wash

Neither option is badly exposed; the differences are marginal.

Where A is marginally better

Channel: both Thu 16 & Sun 19 are LeShuttle peak days, but a Thu evening is calmer than a Sun-morning changeover (FlexiPlus hedges both). French roads: A drives Calais→Stuttgart on Fri 17 (lighter); Sat 18 is Bison-Futé red but that's southbound Med, not our NE line.

Where B is arguably better

The Alps: A crosses the Fernpass/Brenner on Sunday — lorry-free, but heavy with holiday cars and the Tyrol side-roads are shut (can't dodge a jam). B crosses Monday — lighter car volume (weekday), though truck-metering runs on the Brenner corridor. Sunday is not clearly the better day.

The one hard rule both respect: never cross the Fernpass/Brenner on a Saturday (A = Füssen foothills only that day; B = home). That's also the stop-and-go type behind the family's past rear-end. Sources: LeShuttle FlexiPlus peak dates · Bison Futé/Mappy 2026 · ADAC & ÖAMTC Stauprognose.

🛂

EES — cross on the Hungarian passports

The EU Entry/Exit System is live (partial at the tunnel, queue risk). EU citizens are exempt → enter Schengen on the HU passports (EU lane, no biometrics); carry UK passports for UK re-entry.

🟢

Stuttgart green sticker

Both options overnight in Stuttgart, whose whole-city Umweltzone needs a green Umweltplakette on the windscreen — foreign cars too — or €80 + €25. Buy ahead (~€5–15); the Kodiaq qualifies.

🎟️

Vignettes — NOT the night before

An Austrian digital vignette is only valid 18 days after online purchase (unless waived) — buy it now, or grab a physical sticker at the border. Same care for SI & HU. Fernpass B179 is vignette-free; Brenner (AT A13 + IT A22) is a separate toll.

🚧

Fernpass — roadworks, not a blocker

Tunnel approved but built 2027+ (toll not before 2029) — no closure this summer. But Lermoos-tunnel works + a 50 km/h limit mean slowdowns on the B179 on top of the usual jams.

🅿️

ZTL — auto-fines

Don't drive into Bolzano or Trento old-town ZTLs (cameras fine you by post). Stay on the bypass; Predazzo/Val di Fiemme is fine.

🚛

2026 HGV bans (verify near the date)

Italy: Sat 08:00–16:00, Sun 07:00–22:00. Austria: Alpine-pass weekend limits. Hungary: Sat 15:00 → Sun 22:00. Sundays are best for big motorway miles.

🧰

EU driving kit

UK sticker on the car, hi-vis vest per occupant, warning triangle, headlamp beam deflectors, first-aid kit. Add a Crit'Air sticker only if routing through French ZFE cities (Reims/Metz/Strasbourg).

🛏
