The line, in four facts

The F1 discovery

Hungarian GP, Hungaroring, 24–26 Jul. Arrival traffic hits A4 Nickelsdorf → M1 Budapest from Thu 23 — poisoning the classic northern entry exactly on our dates. The S7 southern entry never touches it.

🆕

The S7 back door

Opened Sep 2025: A2 (Graz) → S7 → Heiligenkreuz → M80 → road 8 → Veszprém — skips Vienna, Budapest, the M1 works and the M0 entirely. Csopak sits 20 min below Veszprém. Built for this trip.

🛂

EES — a non-issue right now

EES biometric checks aren't live at Eurotunnel yet — manual registration only, no biometrics taken (Jul 2026), so there are no EES queues to dodge. We're on the Hungarian passports anyway (EU lane); UK passports ride along for UK re-entry. Re-check before travel in case it switches on.
