The call

The Tuesday half-day head-start — gentlest days, smallest Friday.

Cancelling the Tue office visit and taking a half day is what makes this work: the ~2h45 jump to Châlons on Tuesday afternoon lets the heavy driving fall on the calm midweek days (Tue PM / Wed / Thu) and shrinks Friday — the week's worst getaway day and the F1-arrival day — to a short ~3h S7 hop.
