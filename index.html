Don't get caught

Black Saturday + the border kit

Summer Saturdays — especially 18 & 25 Jul — are changeover hell on the arteries; Sundays are lorry-free. The plan is built to sidestep the worst of it.

⚫

Black Saturday — and how the plan dodges it

FR samedi noir / DE Reisewelle / IT esodo: the peak-summer changeover when Europe swaps over at once. The main risk points are the southbound French Med arteries and the Alpine passes.

Sat 18 — deliberately off the Brenner

A short Stuttgart → Füssen hop (~3h), then the afternoon free at Neuschwanstein / the lakes. The Alps are not crossed on Black Saturday — that's the whole point of the Füssen night.

Fri 17 & Sun 19

Fri's Calais → Stuttgart leg is German-autobahn NE transit, not the southbound Med jam. The Fernpass/Brenner crossing falls on the lorry-free Sunday. Return (Tue 1 Sep) clears the August waves.

The rule: cross the Fernpass/Brenner off-Saturday. It's also the exact stop-and-go congestion type behind the family's past rear-end — worth dodging.

🎟️

Vignettes — buy before each border

Online the night before: Austria (10-day), Slovenia, Hungary (10-day D1). Germany is toll-free for cars.

🅿️

ZTL — auto-fines

Don't drive into Bolzano or Trento old-town ZTLs (cameras fine you by post). Stay on the bypass; Predazzo/Val di Fiemme is fine.

🚛

2026 HGV bans (verify near the date)

Italy: Sat 08:00–16:00, Sun 07:00–22:00. Austria: Alpine-pass weekend limits. Hungary: Sat 15:00 → Sun 22:00. Sundays are best for big motorway miles.
