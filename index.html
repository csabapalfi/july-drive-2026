Why Tuesday, and what we're dodging

The week's traffic shape

Tue 21 ≈ Wed 22 calmest · Mon 20 moderate (NRW day-1 + Monday lorry-return) · Thu 23 avoid on the northern route (F1 build). NRW's wave crested the 17–19 weekend; BW + Bavaria don't break up until 30 Jul / 3 Aug. The diaspora ("sıla yolu") wave shares the M1 corridor — not our S7 line.

Dodged by route

Cologne freight funnel (Europe's busiest) · Frankfurt A3 3-lane closure (from Wed 22) · A8 Stuttgart–Munich (ADAC's #1 jam corridor) · Vienna ring · M1 2+1+1 contraflow (+30–60 min, to Sep) · M0 ring · Nickelsdorf F1 crush. Remaining pinch: A3 Regensburg→Passau resurfacing — transit midday Wed.

The standing rule: stop-and-go congestion + fatigue is the family's accident lever (the past rear-end). This plan's worst exposure is one midday roadwork zone — everything else is free-flowing weekday motorway.
