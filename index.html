Where things stand

The decision, in four facts

New info tonight reshaped this. Rotterdam fell away; money and trip-length came to the front.

💸

Six nights would wreck us

The full Med route needs ~6 paid hotel nights. With the summer line already brushing the £10k annual ceiling, that's the real constraint.

🧒

Kids at the perfect age

Erik 11, Ella 9 — old enough to remember Monaco, Pont du Gard and Rovinj, young enough to still love a family road trip. This window won't last.

👵

Rotterdam — a real option

Mum's there 17–20 Jul (firm) and your brother lives there. You'll also see them in Hungary + at SZIN, so it's not a must — but a gathering at his home is a different thing, and it's catchable on the way out.

📍

Fixed anchors

Le Shuttle 17 Jul (Flexiplus) · Csopak booked 24–27 Jul · Zánka 15–20 Aug · SZIN 26–30 Aug · back for Ark Pioneer 3 Sep.

🧭

North or south — not both

Only 7 days from Calais (17 Jul) to Csopak (24 Jul). Rotterdam is ~2h north; the Med loop runs far south (Rotterdam→Avignon ~10h). Combining them lands you at Csopak ~26 Jul — past the booking. So Rotterdam + Med isn't on the table.

🛏️

Free beds with friends?

Basel friends sit on the Swiss route (E); friends near Salzburg (Seewalchen/Attersee) on a Linz-side central route. A hosted night each could knock ~£150–250 off — but only if they're home and can host. Ask first before it changes the plan.

The call — with live numbers

B for the Med · C if cash wins

Live pricing confirms your instinct that beds make or break this. The Med routes now cost £2.2–4.0k to drive — B (£1,260 beds, Milan's low season) is the value Med pick and beats coastal A by ~£600–800. The Swiss E lands about the same as B for one fewer night, because Lucerne (~£400) eats the saving — so E only wins if your Basel friends host.

The honest headline: central C is ~£1,300–2,600 cheaper than any Med route (~£1k all-in), now Munich-free and clear of the transit jams. With summer already brushing the £10k annual ceiling, C — or D if the Rotterdam + Seewalchen beds come free — is the budget-safe call. And every Med route rides on the Rovinj 2-night booking: check it's actually available before you commit to A, B or E.
